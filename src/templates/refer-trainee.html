NetQwix is a brand new teaching platform connecting enthusiasts like you with highly qualified experts for LIVE one-on-one training sessions conducted on NetQwix's customized LIVE interactive meeting platform.

NetQwix is available via any smartphone, computer or tablet with a webcam and reliable Internet access.

NetQwix — How it Works:

1Expert or Enthusiast records swings while playing or practicing.
2Enthusiast joins NetQwix and uploads and labels video clips of pre-recorded footage in any format.
3Enthusiast searches for their favorite Expert and requests a session.
4Sessions can be booked in advance or Experts can be booked instantly — NetQwix will contact the Expert for immediate confirmation.
5When the Expert accepts the session, the Enthusiast is asked to purchase the lesson.
6Payment prompts NetQwix to provide a unique meeting link where the Expert and Enthusiast are magically connected inside NetQwix's custom Live Session portal.
7NetQwix's system enables the Expert to diagram and analyze selected footage live while the Enthusiast watches and they discuss. The system also uses the phone cam/webcam and microphone so changes can be implemented by the Enthusiast under the watchful eye of their Expert.
8The Sessions, as well as clips of all footage uploaded by the Enthusiast, are available for review in their personalized locker.
9A printable Game Plan reflecting the Session experience will be generated by the Expert and placed in the Enthusiast's locker for future reference.

Enthusiasts receive a Locker from which they can play uploaded swings, watch recordings of their lessons, and view each Game Plan generated. Contact The NetQwix Team at 212-829-8749 to get started!
